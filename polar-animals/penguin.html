Penguin

The specie can only found in the southern hemisphere. The evolution didn't gave the the ability to fly but instead gave them swimming abilities. They hold some pretty interesting features and are probably the most famous Antarctic animals.

Friendly

Since most of the penguins are not prayed by animals on ground, they aren't scared from humans.

Range

In the Antarctic (mainly), New Zealand, South America and South parts of Africa, Australia.

Species

Larger species habit colder areas since their bodies can cope with the low temperatures. Smaller penguins inhabit warmer areas

Diet

Seafood. Those who are closer to the equator eat fish and those who are near to Antarctica - squid and krill.

Breeding

Mostly during spring and summer. They breed in large colonies of hundreds penguins.

Underwater

They spent more than half of their lifetime underwater.

Kidnap

If an emperor penguin mother finds her child dead, she is likely to kidnap an unrelated chick.

Hearing

With excellent hearing parents identify their chicks when they call.

Lifespan

15 to 20 years. It depends what specie is the penguin.

Enemies

Mostly prayed by leopard seal and rarely by sharks and killer whales.

Gender

Most of the them aren't sexually dimorphic. Which means males and females look alike.

Swimming

Penguins can hold their breath and stay underwater for about 10 minutes.

Emperor Penguin VS Little Penguin

Emperor penguin is the tallest and heaviest among the penguin's species. It inhabits the Antarctic and isn't prayed by any animal on ground. On the other hand, little penguin inhabits south coasts of Australia and New Zealand . Unlike Emperor penguin, it is prayed on ground from cats, dogs, rats, foxes, large reptiles ect. Although these disadvantages, little penguins population is about 1 million while Emperor's is 600 000. That might be because little penguins are more likely to receive help from humans, than emperors due to the more hospitable environment they inhabit. Some organizations even offer adopting a little penguin, donation and ect.
  • Largest by size
  • Inhabits harsh environment
  • Not prayed on ground
  • Less population
  • Less likely to receive human help
  • Smallest by size
  • Inhabits milder environment
  • Is prayed on ground
  • Higher population
  • Likely to receive human help
