Polar regions

They distinguish from other regions due to low temperatures. In these extreme conditions live animals who have successfully adapted. They're interesting to explore. Some of them even cute.

Snowy Owl

Unlike other owl species, this one copes with extreme weather. Snowy owls are found only in the Arctic. Their magnificent look and surviving in very low temperatures makes them majestic.

Polar Bear

Massive, strong, with killing instincts, they're tough predators. They at the top of arctic food chain. So their diet is completely meat based. Polar bear is Frightful for everyone. But tender and loving for it's sweet cubs.

Arctic Fox

Tiny arctic foxes live in extreme environment. The competition for food is brutal for small animals. But they cope with pray lack using excellent sensing. This makes them excellent hunters.

Penguin

They are birds who have adjusted to swimming instead of flying. Thus they catch their pray underwater. Penguins can be found outside of Antarctica and hold unique features
