Polar regions

Polar regions distinguish from other regions mainly due to the low temperatures and rough environment. However, in these extreme conditions live animals who have successfully adapted to them and are interesting to explore. Some of them even cute.

Snowy Owl

Unlike most of the owls this one copes with extreme weather. Snowy owls are native to the arctic, have thick fur and noble appearance. The magnificent look and ability to survive in very low temperatures makes them majestic yet adorable.

Polar Bear

Massive, strong, with killing instincts, polar bears are tough predators in a harsh environment. They are at the top of the food chain and their diet is completely meat based. Frightful and terrifying yet tender and loving for their sweet precious cubs.

Arctic Fox

Being a tiny animal in an environment with extremely low temperatures, lack of food and tough predators isn't an easy job. Small and clever, Arctic fox manages to resist the harsh environment and continue to be interesting to explore and adorable.

Penguin

They are classified as birds, but they have adjusted to swimming rather than flying. They are excellent swimmers which helps them catch their pray. It's common knowledge that penguins inhabit Antarctica but that's not true about some species.
