Penguins are classified as birds, but they have adjusted to swimming rather than flying. They are excelent swimmers which helps them catch their pray. It's common knowledge that penguins inhabit Antarctica but that's not true about some species.

Friendly

Since most of the penguins are not prayed by animals on ground, they aren't scared from humans.

Penguins can be found in the Antarctic (mainly), New Zeland, South America and South parths of Africa, Austalia.

Species

Larger species habit colder areas since their bodies can cope with the low temperatures. Smaller penguins inhabit warmer areas.

Diet

Seafood. Those who are closer to the equater eat fish and those who are near to Antarctica - squid and kril.

Breeding

Mostly during spring and summer. They breed in large colonies of hundreds penguins.

Kidnap

If an empereor penguin mother finds her child dead, she is likely to kidnap an unrelated chick.

Hearing

Penguins have an excelent hearing. Parent identify his/her chicks by their calls.

Lifespan

15 to 20 years. It deppends what specie is the penguin.

Enemies

They are mostly prayed by leopard seal and rarely by sharks and killer whales.

Gender

Most of the penguins aren't sexually dimorphic. Whick means males and females look alike.

Swimming

Penguins can hold their breath and stay underwater for about 10 minutes.

Underwater

Penguins spent more than half of their lifetime underwater.
